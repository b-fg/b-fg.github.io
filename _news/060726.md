Matthias Claeys has recently finished his Master's thesis in the group. His work involved the acceleration of temporal integration in a CFD solver using neural ODEs.

The thesis can be found at [TU Delft digital library](https://repository.tudelft.nl/record/uuid:39dd3adc-9c14-460d-9253-14b45d6679cb).

Matthias will now continue his academic journey as a PhD student at Imperial College London. We wish him all the best!
