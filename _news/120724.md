This week I attended JuliaCon 2024 in Eindhoven, a great occasion to meet the Julia community and catch up on the latest developments of the language. Together with [Gabriel Weymouth](https://weymouth.github.io/), we presented the new developments of [WaterLily.jl](https://github.com/WaterLily-jl/WaterLily.jl), our fluid simulator written in pure Julia. The talk recording is available [here](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=FwMh2rq9kOU).
