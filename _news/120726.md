Since April 2023, I organize the Large-Scale Computational Fluid Dynamics (LSCFD) seminar series: online seminars on numerical methods, high-performance computing, and scale-resolving simulations. We have hosted 13 seminars so far, with speakers such as Gabriel Weymouth, Jane Bae, Andrea Beck, Soledad Le Clainche, Adrián Lozano-Durán, Freddie Witherden, and Lin Fu.

The recordings are available on the [LSCFD YouTube channel](https://www.youtube.com/@LSCFD1).
