The Burgers Symposium, organized by the [J.M. Burgerscentrum](https://www.tudelft.nl/jmburgerscentrum), is the national fluid dynamics conference in the Netherlands. The symposium is aimed for PhD and postdocs, and is a great place to meet local Dutch researchers in our field!
