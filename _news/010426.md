Evan van der Weide has recently finished his Master's thesis in the group. His work involved the numerical modelling of the Sarsia tubulosa jellyfish using [WaterLily.jl](github.com/WaterLily-jl/WaterLily.jl).

The thesis can be found at [TU Delft digital library](https://repository.tudelft.nl/record/uuid:341ea62d-250a-49dc-a6af-fe81bd77a627). A [short article](https://www.tudelft.nl/en/me/education/student-stories/a-realistic-3d-model-to-understand-jellyfish-swimming-efficiency) was also highlighted on the news of the Faculty of Mechanical Engineering at TU Delft.
