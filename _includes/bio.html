***Welcome!*** I am Dr. Bernat Font (he/his), Postdoctoral Researcher of the [Large-scale Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD) Group](https://www.bsc.es/discover-bsc/organisation/research-departments/large-scale-computational-fluid-dynamics) at the Barcelona Supercomputing Center focusing on turbulence modelling, deep learning and data-driven models, numerical methods, and high-performance computing. Some of my current projects involve:
* Scale-resolving simulation for high-speed flows using low- and high-order methods * Data-driven LES wall models * Deep reinforcement learning for active flow control * Physics-based machine learning for flow field interpolation * `Flower.jl`, a flux reconstruction high-order solver written in Julia
I advocate for open-science. Most of my papers have an e-print version you can download for free. The codes I write are also open-source, and you can find them in [my Github repository](https://github.com/b-fg).
