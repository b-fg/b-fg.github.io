*Welcome!* This is the **Data-Informed Computational Fluid Dynamics** (DI-CFD) group led by Dr. Bernat Font (he/his), Assistant Professor at [TU Delft](https://www.tudelft.nl/staff/b.font/). Our main research interest focuses on exploiting data to improve current CFD methods in terms of computing, modelling, and flow control. Some of our current projects involve:
* Deep reinforcement learning for active flow control * Scale-resolving simulations (SRS) of flows at high Reynolds numbers * Acceleration of SRS using numerical methods enhanced with physics-based machine learning (PBML) * Wall modelling of non-equilibrium boundary layers using PBML * Discovery of partial differential equations from raw data using ML and sparse regression * [WaterLily.jl](https://github.com/weymouth/WaterLily.jl), a Julia CFD solver using finite-volume and immersed-boundary methods with backend-agnostic execution (CPU/GPU)
We advocate for open science. Most of the papers have an e-print version you can download for free. The code is also open source, and you can find them in [GitHub](https://github.com/b-fg). Please feel free to get in touch for questions, suggestions, or collaborations.
