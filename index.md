<p style="text-align: justify"> 
<b>Welcome!</b> I am Bernat Font Garcia, currently a PhD Candidate of the <a href="https://www.southampton.ac.uk/engineering/research/groups/afm.page" class="text_link">Aerodynamics and Flight Mechanics group</a> at the University of Southampton (UK) aiming to graduate in 2020. My PhD is in Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD) and in this personal webpage (still under construction) you will find a summary of my research and interests.</p>

<p style="text-align: justify"> 
I am currently based at the  <a href="https://www.a-star.edu.sg/ihpc/Research/Fluid-Dynamics-FD" class="text_link">Fluid Dynamics group</a> of the Institute of High Performance Computing (Singapore) as part of a collaboration programme between the University of Southampton and the Agency for Science, Technology and Research (A*STAR).</p>

<p style="text-align: justify">
My PhD is focused on studying and modelling the complex physics encountered on the wake of a circular cylinder at high Reynolds $\left(Re\right)$ regimes. In particular, <a href="/3D_to_2D" class="text_link">we have recently</a> investigated the transition from 3-D to 2-D turbulence on the wake of flow past a circular cylinder at $Re=10^4$. On-going research is set on developing a low-dimensional data-driven model for the turbulent wake.</p>
